About the college

Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) is a public technical and research university located in Powai, Mumbai. Established in 1958, it is one of India's oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions, consistently ranked #1 by NIRF. The campus spans 550 acres with world-class research facilities, incubation centres, and 17 academic departments.

IIT Bombay is known for its strong alumni network in Silicon Valley, top-tier placements, and a vibrant campus culture with fests like Mood Indigo and Techfest attracting lakhs of students every year.
